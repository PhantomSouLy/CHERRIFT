# Next content pass

The following systems are ready to receive the next content update:

- World 3 Savanna final content
- World 4 Tropical
- World 5 Ruined Forest
- Mage Cherry
- Archer Cherry
- additional Common Cherry skins
- one additional Epic Cherry skin
- PixelLab art for Blossom Gem, chests, stones, cores and food items

Chest reward pools read directly from `CHERRIFT_DATA.skins`, so newly registered Common, Rare and Epic skins automatically become eligible for their matching chest pools.
Stage chest drops use the stage `world` number, so Worlds 4 and 5 automatically activate the prepared Epic Chest drop chances.
